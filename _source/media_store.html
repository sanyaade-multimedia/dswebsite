You can even enter your own custom text message and have it scroll through any screen division. Learn more

free for everyone

Want to integrate Live TV into a selected screen division? no problem. The mediaTV component supports full HD analog and digital input. External Link - load content from anywhere

free for everyone

Dont want to host your content with us? No hard feelings. The best part is that all your content is still cached on the local SignagePlayer, so an interruption in the connection does not interrupt your audience. Learn more
